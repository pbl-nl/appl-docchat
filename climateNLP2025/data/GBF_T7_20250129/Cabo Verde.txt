Government: Cabo Verde

National target title: Reduce pollution to levels that are not harmful to biodiversity

Description: By 2030, identify, control sources and reduce pollution to levels that are not harmful to biodiversity and the normal ecosystem functioning

Main policy measures: A1. Develop or review and strengthen the implementation of policies and regulations to control sources of pollution A2. Develop relevant policies and measures for the management of hazardous or chemical waste A3. Develop and implement an environmental quality monitoring system
