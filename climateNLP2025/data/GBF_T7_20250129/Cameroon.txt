Government: Cameroon

National target title: Significantly reduce the impact of multiform pollution on biodiversity and ecosystem services, in particular plastic waste, nutrients, pesticides and other toxic and/or hazardous chemicals.

Description: This objective aims to significantly reduce the harmful impacts of various pollutants on biodiversity and ecosystem functions and services , focusing on specific pollutants such as plastics, nutrients, pesticides and toxic chemicals, in order to preserve and restore ecosystems.
