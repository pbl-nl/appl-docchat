Government: Ethiopia

National target title: Eth - Target 7: By 2030, pollution of major ecosystems is reduced by at least 10% from the existing 10 million tones by application of innovative measures, with emphasis on municipal and industrial sources.

Description: Municipal, industrial and agricultural pollution are the prominent drivers affecting biodiversity in Ethiopia. Therefore, this target is set to reduce the impacts of pollution on biodiversity of the country.

Main policy measures: nan

Aspects of the goal or target are covered: This target is highly aligned with GBF Goal A and GBF Target T07 which emphasizes key elements such as reduce pollution risks and negative impact of pollution from all sources, reducing excess nutrients lost to the environment, risks from pesticides and highly hazardous chemicals, preventing, reducing, and working towards eliminating plastic pollution and integrated pest management , and level of quantified commitment for implementation.
