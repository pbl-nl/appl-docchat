Government: Jordan

National target title: By 2030, Jordan will have mitigated the threats posed by hazardous chemicals and pollutants to biodiversity, human health, and ecosystem services and functions. This will be achieved through enhanced management of pesticide use, increased adoption of biocontrol agents and bio-stimulants, implementation of integrated pest management practices, working towards the elimination of plastic pollution, and phasing out the use of highly hazardous chemicals.
