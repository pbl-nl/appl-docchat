National target title: Target 7: Reducing pollution from all sources impacting biodiversity and ecosystem services

Description: nan

Main policy measures: Reducing national emissions of certain air pollutants that may impact biodiversity; Identification and designation of areas vulnerable to nitrates from agricultural sources; Regulating the uses of persistent organic pollutants that may impact biodiversity; Ensuring the protection of aquatic ecosystems against nitrate pollution from agricultural sources;
