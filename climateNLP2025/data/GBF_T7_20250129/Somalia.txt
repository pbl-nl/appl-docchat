Government: Somalia

National target title: Pollution Reduction to Protect Key Ecosystems

Description: By 2030, reduce pollution, including excess nutrients, to levels that are not harmful to biodiversity in Somalia, focusing on reducing runoff into key ecosystems such as rivers and coastal areas, and implementing community-led waste management programs.

Main policy measures: nan

Aspects of the goal or target are covered: Reducing pollution to levels that are not harmful to biodiversity is essential for maintaining healthy ecosystems in Somalia. Focusing on reducing runoff into key ecosystems such as rivers and coastal areas, and implementing community-led waste management programs will mitigate pollution impacts and enhance environmental quality.
