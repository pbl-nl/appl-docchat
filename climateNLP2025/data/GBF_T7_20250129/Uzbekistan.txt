Government: Uzbekistan

National target title: Reduce Pollution to Levels That Are Not Harmful to Biodiversity

Description: Reduce pollution risks and the negative impact of pollution from all sources by 2030, to levels that are not harmful to biodiversity and ecosystem functions and services, considering cumulative effects. Including pollution originating from agriculture (nutrients from fertilizer, pesticides, saline water), from industry (chemicals), and waste (sewage, other waste, particularly plastics).
